Codebook

The file is made of 6 columns; the first five columns represent the five
members of the team and the sixth one is the outcome of the match.

- Professions: The professions are mapped to integers. These are the values: 
 
No player -> 0
Elementalist -> 1
Engineer ->	2
Guardian ->	3
Mesmer -> 4
Necromancer -> 5
Ranger -> 6
Thief -> 7
Warrior	-> 8

- Outcome of the match
0 -> team won
1 -> team lost
